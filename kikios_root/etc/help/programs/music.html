Music Player

Audio player for MP3 and WAV files.

Features

  • Plays MP3 and WAV formats
  • Album/track browser
  • Play, pause, stop controls
  • Progress bar
  • Volume slider

Usage

  • Browse albums in left panel
  • Click album to see tracks
  • Double-click track to play
  • Use playback controls at bottom

Music Location

Place audio files in /music directory. Organize by album folders.

Launch

Click Music icon in dock, or: /bin/music
