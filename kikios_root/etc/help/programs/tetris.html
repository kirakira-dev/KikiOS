Tetris

Classic falling blocks puzzle game.

Objective

Fill complete rows to clear them. Don't let blocks reach the top.

Controls

Left/Right: Move piece
Up/W: Rotate piece
Down/S: Soft drop
Space: Hard drop
Q: Quit
R: Restart

Scoring

More points for clearing multiple rows at once.

Launch

/bin/tetris (runs in terminal)
